# 📊 Data Scientist Salary Dashboard

An interactive **Tableau Dashboard** that provides comprehensive insights into Data Scientist salaries across the globe. This dashboard helps users explore salary trends based on experience level, employment type, company size, job title, and geographic location.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **Data Scientist Salary Dashboard** is designed to analyze and visualize global salary data for professionals in the data science field. It enables stakeholders, job seekers, recruiters, and analysts to gain meaningful insights into how salaries vary across different dimensions such as country, experience, and employment type.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze average Data Scientist salaries worldwide.
- Compare salaries based on **experience level** and **employment type**.
- Identify top countries hiring Data Scientists.
- Understand the distribution of companies by **size** and **location**.
- Provide a clear view of salary differences across **job titles** and **experience levels**.

---

## 🧩 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Filters
- **Job Title**
- **Company Location**

### 🔹 Key Visualizations
1. **Avg Salary by Experience Level & Employment Type**  
   Compares average salaries across Contract, Freelance, Full-Time, and Part-Time roles for different experience levels.

2. **Total Companies by Size and Location**  
   Donut chart showing distribution of companies by size — Small, Medium, and Large.

3. **Avg Salary by Country (Map View)**  
   Geographic visualization of average Data Scientist salaries across the world.

4. **Top 10 Employee Residence**  
   Bar chart highlighting the top 10 countries where Data Scientists are based.

5. **Experience Level Distribution**  
   Donut chart representing the share of Entry, Intermediate, Senior, and Expert level professionals.

6. **Employment Type Distribution**  
   Visual breakdown of employment types in the dataset.

7. **Avg Salary by Job Title & Experience Level**  
   Detailed table showing salary differences across job titles and experience levels.

The dataset contains information about:
